Services

Our service covers multiple disciplines, from banking, insurance and hydro-power to not-for-profit, manufacturing, trading and service industries. We provide the client-focused service model with the breadth of resources. We practice in all major fields of accounting and auditing and offer various professional services which include: financial management; taxation; acquisitions, mergers and reconstructions; IAS/NFRS reporting and internal audit. We can assemble the right team for your business, combining insight from multiple disciplines with extensive business knowledge and industry expertise. We offer a complete service for your business and organization with an assured quality. We adopt a collaborative approach to provide you the benefit of our commercial expertise, local market knowledge, and timely attention to issues which impact your business.
